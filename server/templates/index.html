PNaaS

Project (or Preas) Naming as a Service

Leveraging the quick wit of someone more clever than you.

About
The two hardest problems in computer science are:
  1. Naming things
  2. Cache invalidation
  3. Off by one errors
So you find yourself starting a new project, before you begin you have to name it something thats going to badass. Unfortunately, you are inept in puns, and overall not good very with the word things. Fortunately, there is someone more clever than you. Ask the oracle of naming shit, @joelintheory, to grant you some clevertext. He also accepts cash.
How to use - Abstract
POST some shit to https://pnaas.geocities.io/request with form field desc and owner filled out. The field desc is the description of your poject, owner is who you think you are. You will get your reservation ID back, hit up https://pnaas.geocities.io/retrieve/{resid} to check the status of your lame request. The response status will Open, Responsed, or Updated. Responses will be a list.
How to use - cURL
curl -d owner="Funny Guy" -d desc="A web9.0 enabled store that sells old AOL discs to ferrets" https://pnaas.geocities.io/request
